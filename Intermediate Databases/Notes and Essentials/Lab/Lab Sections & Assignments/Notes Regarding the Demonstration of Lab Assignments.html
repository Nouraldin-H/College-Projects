When accessing the Database Assignments section in Brightspace, you will notice that for every week there is a different lab assignment (consisting of multiple screen shots) that must be submitted, completely and correctly to obtain a grade mark. As noted, each weekly lab assignment is worth either 1, 2, 3, 4 or 5% of your total mark (part marks are permissible). Additionally, students are allowed to submit later lab assignments prior to the week they are due. Any number of “future” lab assignments can be submitted.

  

NOTE: There are two significant deadlines which will be enforced to ensure that students do not too far behind in their lab assignment submissions:

  

DEADLINE #1: Weekly lab assignments from Week #2 to Week #6 inclusive, must be submitted prior to the commencement of the reading week. Once the reading week is in effect, students will not be able to submit these lab assignments.

  

DEADLINE #2: Weekly lab assignments from Week #8 to Week #12 inclusive, must be submitted prior to the commencement of the final assessment week. Once the final assessment week is in effect, students will not be able to submit these lab assignments.
